# PureLoom Naturals
**Handmade. Clean. Comforting.**

---

## Brand Overview

**PureLoom Naturals** creates thoughtfully handmade soaps, soy candles, and perfumes using carefully selected ingredients.  
The brand celebrates simple rituals, clean formulations, and understated luxury inspired by nature.

### Core Values
- Natural & honest ingredients  
- Handcrafted quality  
- Calm, everyday luxury  
- Clean, minimal aesthetics  

---

## Product Categories

- **Handmade Soaps** – gentle, nourishing bars for daily care  
- **Soy Candles** – clean-burning scents for home comfort  
- **Perfumes** – natural-inspired, skin-friendly fragrances  

All products must feel cohesive, minimal, and premium.

# PRODUCT CONTENTS

## Handmade Soaps

### Oatmeal Honey Soap
**Short Description (Benefit + Mood)**  
Soothes dry skin while providing gentle exfoliation and deep nourishment.

**Description**  
A gentle, moisturizing soap enriched with finely ground oatmeal and pure honey to calm irritation and restore skin balance. Leaves skin soft, smooth, and refreshed.

**Ingredients**  
Virgin olive oil, pure coconut oil, castor oil, palmolein oil, sweet almond oil, rice bran oil, ground oatmeal, raw honey, water, salt, sugar, sodium hydroxide.

**Highlights**
- 100% natural and handmade  
- Ideal for dry and sensitive skin  
- Gently exfoliating and deeply moisturizing  
- Free from artificial colorants, fragrances, parabens, sulfates, phthalates, petroleum

## Typography

**Primary Font (Headings / Logo)**  
- Playfair Display (Serif, elegant)

**Secondary Font (Body / Ingredients)**  
- Montserrat or Lato (Sans-serif)

**Rules**
- Product names → Serif  
- Ingredients & highlights → Sans-serif  
- Avoid decorative or script fonts
