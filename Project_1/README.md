# CSS Assignment - PureLoom Naturals

## About PureLoom Naturals

PureLoom Naturals creates thoughtfully handmade soaps, soy candles, and perfumes using carefully selected ingredients. The brand celebrates simple rituals, clean formulations, and understated luxury inspired by nature.

### Core Values
- Natural & honest ingredients

- Handcrafted quality

- Calm, everyday luxury

- Clean, minimal aesthetics

### Product Categories
- Handmade Soaps – gentle, nourishing bars for daily care

- Soy Candles – clean-burning scents for home comfort
  
- Perfumes – natural-inspired, skin-friendly fragrances

- All products must feel cohesive, minimal, and premium.

## Handmade Soaps

### ***Oatmeal Honey Soap***

**Short Description**: Soothes dry skin while providing gentle exfoliation and deep nourishment.

**Description**: A gentle, moisturizing soap enriched with finely ground oatmeal and pure honey to calm irritation and restore skin balance. Leaves skin soft, smooth, and refreshed.

**Ingredients**: Virgin olive oil, pure coconut oil, castor oil, palmolein oil, sweet almond oil, rice bran oil, ground oatmeal, raw honey, water, salt, sugar, sodium hydroxide.

**Highlights**

- 100% natural and handmade

- Ideal for dry and sensitive skin

- Gently exfoliating and deeply moisturizing

- Free from artificial colorants, fragrances, parabens, sulfates, phthalates, petroleum

**Highlights**

## Perfumes

### ***Rose & Oud Perfume***

**Short Description**: Evokes warmth and elegance with a rich, timeless floral-woody depth.

**Description**: A sophisticated blend of rose and oud crafted for those who seek depth and femininity in one. Warm and enveloping, it lingers softly on the skin for a luxurious, all-day experience.

**Ingredients**: Jojoba oil, fractionated coconut oil, rose essential oil, oud fragrance oil, sandalwood essential oil, vitamin E oil.

**Highlights**

- 100% natural base, skin-friendly formula

- Long-lasting, alcohol-free wear

- Ideal for evening and special occasions

- Free from parabens, sulfates, phthalates, synthetic preservatives

### ***Jasmine & Musk Perfume***

**Short Description**: Enhances natural elegance with a soft, sensual, and gently floral sensuality.

**Description**: A refined mix of jasmine and musk, offering an elegant scent that's clean, warm, and ideal for everyday or special occasions.

**Ingredients**: Jojoba oil, fractionated coconut oil, jasmine essential oil, white musk fragrance oil, ylang-ylang essential oil, vitamin E oil.

**Highlights**

- 100% natural base, gentle on sensitive skin

- Soft, intimate projection with lasting wear

- Ideal for everyday use and romantic settings

- Free from parabens, sulfates, phthalates, synthetic preservatives

### ***Vanilla & Amber Perfume***

**Short Description**: Wraps the skin in warmth and softness with a cozy, comforting sweetness.

**Ingredients**: Jojoba oil, fractionated coconut oil, vanilla fragrance oil, amber fragrance oil, cedarwood essential oil, vitamin E oil.

**Highlights**

- 100% natural base, skin-friendly formula

- Warm and long-lasting on the skin

- Ideal for cooler weather and evening use

- Free from parabens, sulfates, phthalates, synthetic preservatives
